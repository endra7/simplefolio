AIRBNB AND ANALYTICS: SOLUTION TO ENHANCE GUEST EXPERIENCE

The objective of the analysis report is to recommend a strategy to enhance the guest safety and meet the guest needs to improve their experiences, all the while increasing the potential revenue to ensure the sustainability of the company.

We used open source datasets from Airbnb (Sydney), BOCSAR (NSW Bureau of Crime Statistics and Research), and Weather report of Sydney Airport to enhance guest experience through safety and targeted ads.

The report proposes the restructuring of Airbnb listing operations and integration of new business model through restricting the suburbs with high crime incidence for the safety of the guests and through seasonal investment on targeted ads for stranded flyers to enhance the guest experience as well as increasing the potential revenue for Airbnb.
